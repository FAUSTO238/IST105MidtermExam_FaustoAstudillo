What I Miss Most from My Home Country

One of the things I miss the most is the food. Ecuadorian cuisine has a unique flavor that I haven't been able to find here. I also miss my family deeply, as I am living alone here, which has been one of the most challenging aspects of adapting to life in Vancouver.

What I Love About Vancouver

The best thing about Vancouver, without a doubt, is how peaceful and safe it is. The city is full of exciting activities and places to explore. I was also amazed by the stunning northern lights, which is something truly magical to witness.
